    Stages

    create_dataset

    Download/build datasets for your recipe.

    collect_stats

    Compute feature shapes and global stats.

    train

    Run Lightning training with `train.yaml`.

    infer

    Write `.scp` outputs under `inference_dir`.

    metric

    Compute metrics from inference outputs.
